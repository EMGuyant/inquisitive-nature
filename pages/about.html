Who I am

I am a naturally inquisitive person who finds himself in a constant state of curiosity. I feed this curiosity by learning and exploring data tools and techniques with a special interest in data preparation and visualization. I thrive on utilizing data analytics, creative thinking, and effective design to distill and communicate the key insights of a dataset. These insights are the fuel for data-driven solutions.
