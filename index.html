What is the Microsoft Power Platform

Category: Quick Start

Tags: Microsoft 365 Power Automate Power Apps Power Platform

The Power Platform is a low-code platform that consists of various applications, tools, and services. The main applications of the Power Platform are Power BI, Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power Virtual Agents. The platform also contains other components to connect to data services, store data in a common data model, and build in artificial intelligence. This post offers an exploratory overview of the Power Platform and will be followed by a series of more deep-dive posts and walkthroughs.
